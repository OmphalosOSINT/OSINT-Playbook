# 📝 Reporting Template

> *Back to [Main Playbook](../README.md)*

---

## Structure recommandée / Recommended Structure

---

### 1. Executive Summary / Résumé exécutif
- Objective of the investigation
- Key findings (3-5 bullet points max)
- Conclusions and recommendations

---

### 2. Scope & Methodology / Périmètre & Méthodologie
- Target(s) investigated
- Timeframe of the investigation
- Tools and sources used
- Legal and ethical framework applied

---

### 3. Findings / Résultats
- Organized by theme or chronology
- Each finding supported by at least one source
- Screenshots and archives as annexes

---

### 4. Analysis / Analyse
- Connections and patterns identified
- Contradictions or gaps noted
- Confidence level for each conclusion :
  - ✅ Confirmed — verified by multiple independent sources
  - ⚠️ Probable — supported by strong indicators
  - ❓ Unconfirmed — single source or unverified

---

### 5. Conclusion
- Summary of key takeaways
- Recommended next steps
- Limitations of the investigation

---

### 6. Sources & References
- All sources listed with URLs and access dates
- Archived versions where available

---

## ⚠️ Reminders / Rappels
- Never include raw PII (Personally Identifiable Information) without necessity
- Always distinguish facts from interpretations
- Date and sign every report
