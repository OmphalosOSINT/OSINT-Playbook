## 🔎 Methodology / Méthodologie

**EN**
1. Identify the target company — full legal name, registration number, country
2. Search national and international company registries (OpenCorporates, Pappers)
3. Map corporate structure — subsidiaries, parent companies, beneficial owners
4. Identify key individuals — directors, shareholders, executives (LinkedIn, registries)
5. Cross-reference with sanctions and PEP lists (OpenSanctions)
6. Search leaked documents databases (OCCRP Aleph, ICIJ Offshore Leaks)
7. Analyze financial data and identify anomalies
8. Document the full corporate network with sources

**FR**
1. Identifier l'entreprise cible — raison sociale complète, numéro d'enregistrement, pays
2. Rechercher dans les registres nationaux et internationaux (OpenCorporates, Pappers)
3. Cartographier la structure corporate — filiales, maisons mères, bénéficiaires effectifs
4. Identifier les personnes clés — dirigeants, actionnaires, executives (LinkedIn, registres)
5. Recouper avec les listes de sanctions et PPE (OpenSanctions)
6. Rechercher dans les bases de documents fuités (OCCRP Aleph, ICIJ Offshore Leaks)
7. Analyser les données financières et identifier les anomalies
8. Documenter l'intégralité du réseau corporate avec sources

## 💡 Tips & Good Practices / Conseils

- Always search for the ultimate beneficial owner (UBO) — not just the registered director
- Shell companies are often layered across multiple jurisdictions — follow each layer
- LinkedIn is underrated for mapping corporate structure and key individuals
- Cross-reference company addresses — the same address shared by many companies is a red flag
- Historical web archives can reveal past directors, products or controversies
- Offshore leaks databases are freely searchable and extremely valuable

---

## 🚩 Key Red Flags / Signaux d'alerte

| Red Flag | Possible Implication |
|----------|---------------------|
| Multiple companies at same address | Shell company network |
| Frequent director changes | Instability or deliberate obfuscation |
| Offshore subsidiaries in secrecy jurisdictions | Tax evasion or money laundering |
| Company appears in sanctions lists | Direct sanctions exposure |
| No public financial filings | Lack of transparency |
